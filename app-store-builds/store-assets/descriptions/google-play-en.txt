INETSHKOLA — Interactive Educational Platform for Students

📚 About:

INETSHKOLA is a modern mobile application for students in grades 0-11 that transforms learning into an exciting adventure! Fully aligned with the Russian school curriculum featuring interactive lessons, games, and tests.

✨ Key Features:

• 📖 12 Grades — Complete curriculum from preparatory group to 11th grade
• 🎮 50+ Educational Games — Math, Russian language, English, Physics, Chemistry, and more
• 📝 Interactive Lessons — Clear explanations with examples and illustrations
• 🏆 Achievement System — Motivation through rewards and badges
• 📊 Learning Progress — Track success in each subject
• 🎯 Tests & Quizzes — Knowledge checks in a game format
